Our Core Expertise

Saree Pre-Pleating & Box Folding Service

⚜

Drape your saree flawlessly in under 2 minutes! No more pins, sliding pleats, or wedding morning stress.

Saree pre-pleating is our core specialized work at Devi Atelier. We custom-draft and adjust the shoulder and waist pleats according to your exact height and body measurements, steam iron them to hold a crisp shape, and pack them neatly in travel-safe designer boxes.

Perfect for brides, bridal parties, wedding guests, and travelers. When it's time to wear, just wrap, pin, and step out looking elegant in minutes!

  • Custom measured based on your height & waist.
  • High-pressure steam ironed to ensure crisp, lasting folds.
  • Shipped in special cardboard folding boxes to preserve pressing.
  • Supports all fabrics: Kanjeevarams, Organzas, Linens, & Cottons.

Custom Accessories

Silk Thread Bangles Designing Service

⚜

Hand-wrapped pure silk thread bangles customized to match your boutique outfit details.

Complete your ethnic attire with our custom thread bangles, another core specialty at Devi Atelier. We select high-quality acrylic bases and wrap them in vibrant silk threads matching the exact shade of your lehenga or saree.

Each set is embellished with premium zircon stone laces, Kundan stones, bead drops, and silk tassels, tailored specifically for brides, bridesmaid groups, and festive occasions.

  • Wrapped in premium silk threads matching your dress color code.
  • Accented with detailed Kundan and zircon stone-work frames.
  • Available in custom sizes ranging from toddler bases to bridal sets.
  • Fast turnaround time matching your event deadlines.

Our Story & Ethos

Meet Devi & Her Creative Vision

⚜

Founded by master designer Devi, our boutique atelier is dedicated to reviving classic tailoring techniques combined with modern visual elegance. We believe that every custom outfit tells a story, and the perfect garment begins with selection of premium materials and exact cuts.

Whether designing a heavy bridal trousseau or a modern pre-stitched party wear saree, we focus on detailed embellishments, sturdy drapes, and flawless tailoring. Every fitting session is personal, ensuring your boutique experience is comforting and results in a design that matches your aesthetic dreams.
